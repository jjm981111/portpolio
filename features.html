Sprit of Ecstasy

The Spirit of Ecstasy is the iconic hood ornament found on Rolls-Royce vehicles. It symbolizes grace, elegance, and forward movement. Originally designed by sculptor Charles Sykes, the Spirit of Ecstasy is often crafted from stainless steel and can be customized to reflect the owner's preferences, such as being illuminated or adorned with precious metals.

Coach Door

The Coach Door, also known as suicide doors, is a distinctive feature found on Rolls-Royce models like the Phantom and Ghost. These rear-hinged doors open in the opposite direction to conventional doors, creating a grand entrance and allowing for easier ingress and egress. The Coach Door design adds to the luxurious and exclusive feel of Rolls-Royce vehicles, making them stand out from other luxury cars.

Customizing

Rolls-Royce offers an extensive range of customization options to ensure that each vehicle is unique and tailored to the owner's preferences. From bespoke paint colors and handcrafted interior trims to personalized embroidery and monograms, the possibilities for customization are virtually limitless. Rolls-Royce's team of skilled craftsmen and designers work closely with customers to create bespoke vehicles that reflect their individual tastes and lifestyles, ensuring that every Rolls-Royce is truly one-of-a-kind.
