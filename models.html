Wraith

Price: Starting at around $330,000 USD (prices may vary based on customization and options).

Fuel Efficiency: Approximately 12 mpg in the city and 18 mpg on the highway.

Engine Information: Equipped with a powerful 6.6-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing around 624 horsepower and 605 lb-ft of torque.

Features: The Wraith boasts a luxurious interior with premium leather upholstery, wood trim, and handcrafted details. It features advanced technology including a large infotainment system with navigation, premium audio system, adaptive cruise control, and a suite of driver assistance features.

Ghost

Price: Starting at approximately $315,000 USD (prices may vary based on customization and options).

Fuel Efficiency: Around 13 mpg in the city and 20 mpg on the highway.

Engine Information: Powered by a 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine delivering approximately 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque.

Features: The Ghost offers a refined interior with premium leather seating, wood veneer trim, and ambient lighting. It comes equipped with a suite of luxury amenities including a high-end audio system, dual-zone automatic climate control, and adaptive air suspension for a smooth ride.

Phantom

Price: Starting at around $450,000 USD for the standard wheelbase and $500,000 USD for the extended wheelbase version (prices may vary based on customization and options).

Fuel Efficiency: Approximately 12 mpg in the city and 20 mpg on the highway.

Engine Information: Powered by a majestic 6.75-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine producing around 563 horsepower and 664 lb-ft of torque.

Features: The Phantom offers unmatched levels of luxury and refinement, with a spacious and opulent interior featuring handcrafted leather upholstery, wood paneling, and exquisite details. It comes equipped with state-of-the-art technology including a panoramic sunroof, rear-seat entertainment system, adaptive cruise control, and a suite of driver assistance features.
